Important Government Schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a government initiative aimed at providing affordable housing to urban and rural poor.
  • Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana 2.0: PMUY 2.0 provides free LPG connections to low-income families, promoting safe cooking practices.
  • PM Kisan Samman Nidhi: PM-KISAN offers financial assistance to small farmers, with Rs. 6,000 per year transferred directly to their bank accounts.
